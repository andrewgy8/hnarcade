## About

Forkle is a free daily word puzzle that puts a new twist on the crossword. Every cell in the grid contains two letters at once - one for the blue layer and one for the yellow layer. You solve both crosswords simultaneously, sharing the same grid. There are two sets of clues, both of which have their own theme, but also have a connection between the two. A new puzzle drops every day. Track your stats, streak or work through the archive, but have fun! No app download needed - plays in the browser on any device.
