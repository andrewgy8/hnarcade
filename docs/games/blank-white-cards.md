## About

Online [1000 Blank White Cards](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1000_Blank_White_Cards) - a social party game where you make the rules!

Setup a multiplayer room with your friends. Play on [Discord](https://discord.com/discovery/applications/1389508624774201395) or any other video chat, or even face to face IRL. Use the [Deck Editor](https://blankwhite.cards/editor) to save, update, and grow your collection over time.

Use the Global Deck for a quick local pass-and-play experience. Create and submit cards there for the whole world to see!
