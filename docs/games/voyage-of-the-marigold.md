# Voyage of the Marigold

Captain a starship through unexplored sectors on a desperate mission. VotM is a (mostly) text-based game where you play as the captain issuing high-level orders to your officers and crew. Designed to play out like a season of (classic) Star Trek, every game is different as the map is randomized.
