## About

Lichess is a free and open-source chess platform. It supports standard chess and variants like Crazyhouse, Chess960, King of the Hill, and more. Features include puzzles, tournaments, analysis boards, studies, and a computer opponent. Entirely funded by donations and built by a community of volunteers.
